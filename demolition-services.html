Our Demolition Services

"Precision Demolition Services for Every Project"

At CLEVER CREATE, we offer a range of specialized demolition services tailored to meet the needs of complex projects. From precision dismantling to large-scale demolitions, we prioritize safety, efficiency, and environmental sustainability. Explore our comprehensive demolition services below:

Concrete Core Cutting

Precision Coring for Structural Modifications

We offer precise core cutting for a variety of applications, including electrical, plumbing, and HVAC. Our diamond drilling technology can create holes from 12mm to 600mm in diameter. Whether for small or large openings, we ensure precise cuts at any angle. We also offer deep-hole and angular drilling options for more complex needs. Trust CLEVER CREATE for precision and efficiency in core cutting.
Services Offered:

  • Coring for cables and pipes
  • Creating ventilation openings
  • Deep-hole and angular diamond drilling
  • Test core extraction with diamond drilling
  • Securing chemical anchors in steel structures
  • Core drilling with epoxy injection for reinforcement rods

Concrete Wall Saw Cutting

Accurate Cuts for Clean Openings

Our wall saw cutting services provide clean, precise cuts for reinforced concrete and masonry walls. Ideal for doorways, windows, and lift shafts, we achieve cutting depths of up to 750mm. Our dust-free, low-noise operation ensures minimal disruption to surrounding areas. Safety is prioritized through careful planning and operator supervision. With smooth finishes, our cuts maintain the structural integrity of your building. Services Offered:

  • Cutting reinforced concrete walls up to 750mm deep
  • Creating precise openings for doors and windows
  • Adjusting wall heights and shapes
  • Storage and inventory space optimization
  • Lift-shaft openings with smooth, non-overlapping cuts
  • Dust-free and low-noise cutting environment for safety

Concrete Scanning Services

Detecting Hidden Dangers Before Drilling or Cutting

Before any cutting or drilling, we utilize advanced ground-penetrating radar (GPR) to detect hidden structures. Our scanning service helps avoid risks like cutting into rebar, pipes, or post-tensioned cables. Real-time imaging ensures that you make informed decisions before proceeding. This non-destructive technique improves safety and reduces costly mid-project repairs. CLEVER CREATE guarantees accurate detection for seamless operations. Services Offered:

  • Detection of conduits, rebar, pipes, and cables
  • Identifying slab thickness and voids
  • Real-time scanning for metallic and non-metallic objects
  • Minimizing risks of cutting through post-tensioned cables or utilities
  • Enhanced safety and prevention of costly mid-project repairs
